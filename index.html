Recommended: install Ollama for the most accurate score

The scorer's semantic analysis uses a small embedding model via Ollama (lightweight — runs on almost any machine, installs the model automatically). Without it, scores use a simpler offline method and will vary, so installing Ollama is strongly recommended.

Rewriting needs no setup: after scoring you get a ready-to-paste ChatGPT prompt. Rewriting inside the app with a local chat model is a separate, optional feature (off by default) — for advanced users with a capable machine.

🍎 Opening it on a Mac — first time only

The app is safe; it just isn't registered with Apple, so macOS asks you to approve it once. It takes about 30 seconds:

  1. Unzip the download: double-click ATS-Resume-Scorer-macos.zip. You'll get the app ATS-Resume-Scorer. (Optional: drag it into your Applications folder.)
  2. Double-click the app. macOS will say “Apple could not verify…” — click Done. (Do not click “Move to Trash”.)
  3. Open the menu → System Settings → Privacy & Security.
  4. Scroll down to Security. You'll see “ATS-Resume-Scorer was blocked…” next to an Open Anyway button — click Open Anyway.
  5. Enter your Mac password or Touch ID, then click Open Anyway once more.
  6. ✅ The app starts and opens in your browser. You won't have to do this again.

Shortcut on older macOS: instead of the steps above, you can right-click (or Control-click) the app → Open → Open.

🪟 Opening it on Windows — first time only

  1. Double-click ATS-Resume-Scorer-windows.exe.
  2. If a blue “Windows protected your PC” screen appears, click More info → Run anyway. (One time only.)
  3. ✅ The app opens in your browser. Keep the small black window open while you use it.

Quitting the app

Click the ⏻ Quit button in the top-right corner of the app — it stops the app cleanly. (On Windows you can also just close the small black window.)
